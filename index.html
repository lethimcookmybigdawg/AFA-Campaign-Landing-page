Fine print

Campaign Rules

  • Only futures trading volume generated within this window is counted toward leaderboard standings and prize pool unlocks. Trades outside the window are excluded.
  • Participants must complete identity verification (KYC) before the campaign closes. Accounts under review, restricted, or in violation of BTCC's Terms of Service at any point during the campaign are disqualified.
  • Both perpetual and dated futures contracts qualify. Spot trades, copy-trading volume routed from leaders, and settlements are excluded. Cross-margin and isolated-margin positions count equally.
  • A trader may rank on both leaderboards, but the Messi-signed jersey grand prize is awarded only to the #1 finisher of the Futures Trading Volume leaderboard. Profit rate prizes are announced separately.
  • Milestones are: 0 → 8,000 USDT, 500M → 50,000, 1B → 100,000, 1.5B → 150,000, 4B → 450,000, 5B → 700,000, and 6B → 1,000,000. The largest unlocked tier reached during the campaign window applies for distribution.
  • If a finishing rank's individual volume falls below the minimum required by the distribution table, that share rolls down to the next eligible rank. See the Prize Pool Distribution Rules table for thresholds.
  • BTCC's risk team reviews leaderboard standings before payout. Suspicious activity — including coordinated trading between related accounts — results in disqualification, forfeiture of rewards, and may trigger account-level enforcement.
  • Sub-accounts inherit their main account's standing and cannot be entered separately. Multiple registrations tied to a single individual (by KYC, device, or payment method) will be merged or disqualified at BTCC's discretion.
  • Eligibility is restricted to retail individual traders. Liquidity providers, institutional clients on negotiated fee schedules, and BTCC employees and their immediate family members are excluded from prize distribution.
  • USDT rewards are credited directly to the futures wallet of the qualifying account. The Messi-signed jersey is shipped to the verified address on file; the champion will be contacted by BTCC support to confirm delivery details.
  • In the event of force majeure, technical failure, or regulatory change, BTCC may modify campaign terms or terminate the campaign without prior notice. All decisions made by BTCC regarding eligibility, rule interpretation, and dispute resolution are final.
